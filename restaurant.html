Our restaurant and award winning team select the finest seasonal and local produce and combine it with their skill & flair offering a wide range of options, for the meat lover, pescitarian, flexitarian, vegetarian or vegan amongst you. A well segmented kitchen assists in keep the foods as free as possible from contamination.

As our kitchen staff come from all over the globe, we can cook your steak, your dahl, your fish and possibly your favourite as you require. Don't be shy or afraid to ask, we will do our best to give you exactly what is craved.
