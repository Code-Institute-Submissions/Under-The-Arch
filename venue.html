~ Our Venue ~

At 800 capacity (standing) & 550 capacity (seated) our venue is a perfect location to catch local & international music acts across a wide variety of genres from acoustic to rock, pop & metal as well as the biggest country stars can be found here.

We also host some of the finest local, national & international DJ's. Superstar DJ's who have previously spun here include Carl Coxz, Sascha & Digweeed, Armind vonBurren.

Drama, classical and theatre groups (local, national & international) also have a place here.
Please sign-up or follow us on social media to keep up to date with who is coming soon.
