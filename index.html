~ About Us ~

Established over 150 years ago and staying within the family through the generations, we went from coach-house to small hotel and now into our 6th generation and our current incarnation of award winning bar, restaurant and brewery / distillery as well as an entertainment hotspot with overnight accomodation and a great breakfast.

Whether it's drinks on the edge of town after a tough week, a few leisurely measures to help shift those midweek blues, an escape for the weekend, food with family & friends, a tour of our widely acclaimed brewery / distillery or a night catching the latest music acts & DJ's or being amazed with the shows in our theatre, the options are many, the choice is ... YOURS.
