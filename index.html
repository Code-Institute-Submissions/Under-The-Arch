About Us

Established over 150 years ago and staying within the family through the generations, we went from coach-house to small hotel and now in our 6th generation, to our current incarnation of an award winning bar, restaurant and brewery / distillery as well as an entertainment hotspot with overnight accomodation and a great breakfast.

Whether it's drinks on the edge of town after a tough week, a few leisurely measures to help shift those midweek blues or an escape for the weekend, for food with family & friends, a tour of our widely acclaimed brewery / distillery or a night catching the latest music acts & DJ's or being amazed with the shows in our theatre, the options are many, the choice is YOURS.
Our Bar's

With 3 bars we can cater for most tastes.

A snug bar for the whiskey & gin connossieur, sample our own distillations or choose from the selction of other fine spirits on offer. Maybe you'd prefer the main bar with it's contemporary yet comfortable surrounding offering our own brews along with a selection of the usual suspects. Or maybe our coffee bar is for you. A place where you can stop for a quick take-away, sit with friends or on your own watching the world pass by. Whichever you choose we will have the coffee you need for you.
Our Restaurant

Our restaurant and award winning team select the finest seasonal and local produce and combine it with their skill & flair offering a wide range of options, for the meat lover, pescitarian, flexitarian, vegetarian or vegan amongst you. A well segmented kitchen assists in keep the foods as free as possible from comtamination.

As our kitchen staff come from all over the globe, we can cook your steak, your dahl or your fish as you require. Do not be afraid to ask, we will do our best to give you exactly what is desired.
Our Venue

At 800 capacity (standing) & 550 capacity (seated) our venue is a perfect location to catch local & international music acts across a wide variety of genres from acoustic to rock, pop & metal as well as the biggest country stars can be found here.

We also host some of the finest local, national & international DJ's. Superstar DJ's who have previously spun here include Carl Coxz, Sascha & Digweeed, Armind vonBurren. Drama, classical and theatre groups (local, national & international) also have a place here. Please sign-up or follow us on social media to keep up to date with who is coming soon.
