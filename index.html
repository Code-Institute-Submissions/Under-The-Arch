~ Our Story ~

Established over 150 years ago and staying within the family through the generations, we went from coach-house to small hotel and now into our 6th generation and our current incarnation of award winning bar, restaurant and entertainment hotspot.

Be it drinks on the edge of town after a tough week, a few quiet drinks to ease those midweek blues, food with family & friends or even a night catching the latest music acts & DJ's or being amazed with the shows in our theatre, theoptions are many, the choice is ... YOURS.
