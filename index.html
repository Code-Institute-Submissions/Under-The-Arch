~ Our Story ~

Established over 150 years ago and staying within the family through the generations, we went from coach-house to small hotel and now into our 6th generation and our current incarnation of award winning bar, restaurant and entertainment hotspot.

Be it drinks on the edge of town after a tough week, a few quiet drinks to ease those midweek blues, food with family & friends or even a night catching the latest music acts & DJ's or being amazed with the shows in our theatre, theoptions are many, the choice is ... YOURS.

With 3 bars we can cater for most tastes.
A snug bar for the whiskey & gin connossieur, sample our local distillations or choose from the selction of other fine spirits on offer.
A sanctuary, a place to sit back, away from the hustle of daily life and enjoy the textures and tastes.

Maybe you'd prefer the main bar with it's contemporary yet comfortable surrounding offering a selection of local craft brews along with a selection of the usual suspects.
There is also a large selection of bar food available daily from 11.30am until 9pm.
(* Not all foods available at all times *)

Or maybe our coffee bar (open 7.30am) is for you. A place where you can stop for a quick take-away, meet with friends or watch the world pass by.
However you choose to spend your time we will have the perfect coffee for you.

Our restaurant and award winning team select the finest seasonal and local produce and combine it with their skill & flair offering a wide range of options for all foodies. For the meat lover, pescitarian, flexitarian, vegetarian or vegan we cater for all.

Be it a fresh salad, a juicy burger, a sizzling stirfry, a mouth-watering steak, fish, vegan & vegetarian options. Ourt chefs hit the markets early to get the best of produce as well as delivery from local farmers and suppliers, we offer the best of locally grown, seasonal foods available.


As our kitchen staff come from all over the globe, we can cook your steak, your dahl, your fish and possibly your favourite as you require. Don't be shy or afraid to ask, we will do our best to give you exactly what is craved.

At 800 capacity (standing) & 550 capacity (seated) our venue is a perfect location to catch a wide variety of local & intl. music acts.
We regularly host some of the finest local, national & intl. artists and DJ's across rock, pop, country & dance.


Singers such as Brezzie, bands like Ashe, Carona's, Republic1, Sam Handwich and the Blizzurd's as well as superstar DJ's like Marc Kavanagh, Mr. Sprung, Dave Pierce, Carl Coxz, Sascha & Digweeed, Armind von Burren, Podge vanDyke, T-esto & Pol Oakenhold have all performed here.


Drama, classical and theatre groups (local, national & intl.) also have a place here.
Performances of the Leaving Cert curriculum drama's are also shown here each year.
